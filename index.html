Artist Talk & Journal-Making Workshop
with

Roberta Shea

Thursday, March 14, 2019 — 6:00 to 7:30 P.M.
Durham Public Library

Throughout the month of March, DPL will be exhibiting Connecticut artist Roberta Shea's most recent paintings, which focus on images inspired by her trip to the Isles of Shoals as well as the life and poetry of Appledore Island's Celia Thaxter.

To accompany Shea's exhibit entitled “A Sea of Flowers,” please join her talk and journel-making workshop where you will learn about the life of Celia Thaxter, her garden, and her Paris salon with Childe Hassam on Appledore Island.

Registration is limited and available at www.durhampubliclibrary.org or via Event Keeper
