C.H. Booth Public Library Presents

An Exhibition of Three Newtown Artists

February 1, 2018 ~ February 28, 2018

S. ADAMS - C. GAULIN-MCKENZIE - ROBERTA SHEA

The Cyrenius H. Booth Library in Newtown will be presenting an Exhibit of Three Newtown Women Artists during the month of February 2018. This exhibit will feature landscape oil paintings by Roberta Shea, acrylic paintings by colorist S. Adams, and figurative collage by C. Gaulin-McKenzie.

The public is cordially invited to attend the opening reception and meet the artists on Sunday, February 4, 2018 from 1:00 to 4:00 pm. Works will be on display throughout the month in the Community Room of the library during regular hours at 25 Main Street in Newtown, CT 06470.
